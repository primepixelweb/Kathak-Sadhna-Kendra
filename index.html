About Our Institute

Welcome to Kathak Sadhna Kendra, a sanctuary for the preservation and propagation of the ancient Indian classical dance form, Kathak. Founded on the principles of devotion (Sadhna) and discipline, our institute has been nurturing talent for over a decade.

Our training goes beyond mere footwork and spins; we dive deep into the cultural roots, expressions (Abhinaya), and rhythmic mastery (Taal) that make Kathak a storytelling masterpiece. Join us to embark on a beautiful journey of grace, rhythm, and tradition.

Our Classes

Beginner Classes

Learn the foundational steps, basic tatkar (footwork), and hand gestures. Perfect for those starting their journey.

Intermediate Training

Focus on complex chakkars (spins), tihais, and introductory expressive pieces (Abhinaya).

Advanced Performance

Rigorous training for stage performances, mastering complex rhythmic cycles and deep storytelling.

Kids Kathak Classes

A fun and engaging curriculum designed specifically to introduce children to Indian culture and rhythm.
